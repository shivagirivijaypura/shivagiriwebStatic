Significance

Shivagiri Vijaypura is not just a site of worship; it is a symbol of devotion, artistry, and cultural heritage. It continues to inspire and attract countless devotees, leaving an indelible mark on all who visit. The towering statue of Lord Shiva stands as a beacon of faith, drawing people from all corners of the world to experience the divine presence and the spiritual tranquility that Shivagiri offers.

Poojas and Offering

At Shivagiri Vijaypura, the day begins with a series of sacred rituals and poojas that honor Lord Shiva. These poojas are performed with utmost devotion and are aimed at invoking the blessings of the deity for peace, prosperity, and well-being. Devotees can participate in these daily poojas or simply observe the rituals to feel the divine presence.

Rudra Abhishekam

Invoke the blessings of Lord Shiva with the powerful Rudra Abhishekam, a sacred ritual performed with devotion and reverence.

Panchamrut Abhishekam

Experience divine bliss with the sacred Panchamrut Abhishekam, a ritual bathing of the deity with five nectars.

Ksheer Abhishekam

Immerse yourself in the divine blessings with Ksheer Abhishekam, a sacred ritual where the deity is bathed in milk.

Vastralankara

Adorn the deity with beautiful garments and accessories in the Vastralankara, enhancing the divine presence.

Mahashivaratri Celebrations

Mahashivaratri is one of the most significant festivals celebrated at Shivagiri Vijaypura. On this auspicious occasion, special poojas and Abhishekams are performed throughout the night. Devotees observe fasts and participate in continuous chanting and prayers. The festival attracts lakhs of devotees who come to seek the blessings of Lord Shiva and experience the spiritual fervor.

Your Special Day Celebrations

Shivagiri Vijaypura provides a unique opportunity to make your special day even more memorable by performing dedicated poojas. Whether it’s a birthday, wedding anniversary, or any other significant milestone, these poojas are designed to invoke blessings and ensure auspiciousness.
